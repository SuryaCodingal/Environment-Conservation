Environment Pollution

Pollution is entrance of harmful substances in our enviornment that cause discomfort to human beings . These can be in the form of chemical substances or energy such as heat or light . Pollutants can be naturally occurring substances or energies , but are considered contaminants when in excess of natural levels. Environmental pollution takes place when the environment cannot process and neutralize harmful by-products of human activities (poisonous gas emissions) in due course without any structural or functional damage to its system. Pollution occurs because of lack of knowledge in human beings of how to decompose waste or unnaturally generated elements.

Why pollution matters

It matters first and for most because it has negative impacts on crucial environmental services such as provision of clean air and clean water without which life on Earth as we know it would not exist. Pollution affects our environment because the water pollution can affect the living conditions of people and plants.

Ways to Reduce Pollution

There are simple steps you can take in your everyday life to help improve air quality -


  • When purchasing a new car, consider one that is the most efficient, lowest-polluting vehicle or even a zero-emission electric car.
  • Replace energy-hungry incandescent lights with compact florescent light bulbs.
  • Recycle paper, plastic, metals and organic materials.
  • Choose products that use recycled materials.
  • Buy electricity generated from renewable energies i.e. hydroelectric, wind or solar power
  • Print and photocopy on both sides of paper
  • Use paper or leather bags instead of plastic bags
  • Quit Smoking
  • If possible Use Fan Instead of AC
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle as much as you can

    • These are some of the ways to reduce pollution.
